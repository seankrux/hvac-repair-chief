# Our HVAC Maintenance Process in Boca Raton

Every HVAC Repair Chief maintenance visit in Boca Raton follows a comprehensive, documented process. We do not rush through a checklist -- we perform a genuine assessment and tune-up of your system.

## Step 1: System Overview and Customer Check-In

Before we begin, we ask about anything you have noticed -- unusual sounds, rooms that seem warmer than others, higher energy bills. Your observations help us focus attention where it matters most.

## Step 2: Outdoor Condenser Service

We inspect and clean the condenser coil using appropriate coil cleaner to remove accumulated dirt and organic buildup. We test the capacitor, contactor, and fan motor. We check refrigerant pressures and electrical connections. We note any signs of wear or developing issues.

## Step 3: Indoor Air Handler Service

We inspect the evaporator coil for dirt and biological growth. We check the blower wheel and motor, inspect the electrical components, and assess the filter. We flush and treat the condensate drain line to prevent clogs -- a critical step in South Florida.

## Step 4: Thermostat Verification

We verify the thermostat is reading accurately, staging correctly, and communicating properly with the air handler and outdoor unit. If you have a smart thermostat, we confirm its settings are optimized for South Florida conditions.

## Step 5: Performance Measurement

We measure supply air temperature, return air temperature, and calculate the temperature differential. This tells us whether the system is producing proper cooling. We compare refrigerant pressures to manufacturer specifications.

## Step 6: Written Service Report

We provide a written summary of everything inspected, any issues found, and our recommendations. Nothing is done without your approval, and all findings are documented for your records.

## Step 7: Honest Recommendations

If we identify components approaching end of life or conditions that should be addressed, we tell you honestly and explain the priority. We never create unnecessary urgency -- we give you the information you need to make informed decisions.
