# AC Installation FAQs — Boca Raton, FL

## How do I know if I need a new AC or just a repair?

If your system is more than 12 to 15 years old, has a failed compressor, uses R-22 refrigerant (now phased out and expensive), or has required multiple major repairs in recent years, replacement usually makes more financial sense. We will give you an honest assessment based on your specific system.

## How long does AC installation take?

A standard residential replacement in Boca Raton takes four to eight hours for most homes. New construction or multi-zone installations may take longer. We minimize disruption to your household and clean up completely before leaving.

## What size AC do I need for my home?

Sizing is based on a Manual J load calculation, which accounts for your home size, ceiling height, window area, insulation levels, and sun exposure. South Florida homes often have unique factors — large glass areas, concrete construction, and intense solar gain — that affect the calculation. Do not let anyone size your system based on square footage alone.

## Do I need a permit for AC installation in Boca Raton?

Yes, most AC replacements in Boca Raton and Palm Beach County require a permit. HVAC Repair Chief coordinates permit applications and inspections as part of the installation process, so you do not have to worry about compliance.

## What brands do you install?

We install and warranty Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Daikin, Goodman, and other leading brands. We recommend the best fit for your home, budget, and energy goals — not the brand with the highest profit margin for us.

## Will a new AC system work with my existing ductwork?

Often yes, but we inspect your ductwork during the estimate process. Leaky or undersized ducts will undermine the performance of even a brand-new system. We can repair or replace ductwork as part of the installation if needed.

## What warranty comes with a new installation?

Manufacturer warranties typically cover parts for 10 years with registration. HVAC Repair Chief also provides a workmanship warranty on the installation itself. We walk you through the warranty terms for your specific equipment.

## How soon can you install a new system?

In most cases, we can schedule installation within a few days of your estimate approval. Emergency replacement situations are given priority scheduling.
