## When to Call for Emergency HVAC Service

Not every AC issue requires an emergency call, but some situations demand immediate attention:

- **Complete cooling failure** -- The system is running but not cooling at all, and indoor temperatures are rising rapidly.
- **AC failure with vulnerable occupants** -- Any loss of cooling when elderly, very young, or medically vulnerable people are in the home.
- **Electrical smell or burning odor** -- A burning smell from the air handler or outdoor unit indicates a potentially dangerous electrical fault.
- **Water flooding from the unit** -- A backed-up condensate drain can overflow and cause rapid water damage to ceilings and walls.
- **System failure during a heat wave** -- When outdoor temperatures are extreme and there is no safe alternative to AC cooling.
